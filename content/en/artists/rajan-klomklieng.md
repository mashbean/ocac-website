Rajan Klomklieng currently lives and works in Ratchaburi, Thailand. Having a western Thailand background of Jombueng region, his work crosses many mediums, including sound art, performance art, street art, text and video work. He has been invited to “*The Rendezvous*”, South East Asia urban art event in Yangon, Myanmar, 2012. He also hosted a backyard music festival in Ratchaburi collaborating with the band Sriracha. He performed sound live in Open Contemporary Bangkok, and attended Bangkok International Design Festival in Bangkok Art and Culture Centre in 2012.
