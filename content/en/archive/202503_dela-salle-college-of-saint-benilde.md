This visit was coordinated by Alain Zedrick, Director of the Creative Industries Management Program at De La Salle-College of Saint Benilde in Manila, Philippines, who led a group of students on a study tour to Taiwan. During the visit, both parties shared insights into the current state and development of the arts sector in the Philippines. This visit served to build a bridge of exchange between young art students from Taiwan and the Philippines.
